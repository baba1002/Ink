What is a logo?

A logo description briefly explains the visual elements and meaning of a logo. It typically includes details about the colors, shapes, fonts, symbols, and the message or values the logo is meant to represent. It’s like a snapshot that conveys the essence of a brand.



What is a Business Card?

A business card is a small, portable card that contains key information about an individual or business. It typically includes details like a name, job title, company name, logo, contact information (phone number, email, address), and sometimes a tagline or website.



What is an Advertising Poster?

An advertising poster is a printed or digital visual designed to promote a product, service, event, or idea. It combines eye-catching imagery, compelling text, and branding elements to grab attention and deliver a message quickly. Advertising posters are widely used in public places like streets, buildings, and bus stops, as well as online, to attract potential customers or audiences.
